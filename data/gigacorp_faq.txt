7  SECTION 1: SHIPPING POLICIES
8  ---------------------------------------------------------------
9
10 Q1.1: What shipping options does GigaCorp offer?
11 A1.1: GigaCorp offers four shipping tiers: Standard Ground ($4.99, 5-7 business
12        days), Expedited ($12.99, 2-3 business days), Overnight Priority ($24.99,
13        next business day by 10:30 AM), and Same-Day Delivery ($39.99, available
14        in select metro areas for orders placed before 11:00 AM local time).
15
16 Q1.2: Is free shipping available?
17 A1.2: Yes. All orders totaling $75.00 or more (before taxes and after any
18        discounts) qualify for free Standard Ground shipping within the contiguous
19        United States. Orders to Alaska, Hawaii, and U.S. territories do not
20        qualify for free shipping and are charged a flat regional surcharge of
21        $14.99 regardless of order size.
22
23 Q1.3: Does GigaCorp ship internationally?
24 A1.3: GigaCorp ships to 47 countries. International Standard shipping costs
25        $19.99 and takes 10-15 business days. International Express shipping costs
26        $44.99 and takes 4-6 business days. Customers are responsible for all
27        import duties, customs fees, and local VAT charges. GigaCorp does not
28        ship to embargoed nations or restricted regions as defined by U.S. export
29        control regulations.
30
31 Q1.4: When will my order ship after I place it?
32 A1.4: Orders placed Monday through Friday before 2:00 PM Eastern Time (ET) are
33        processed and dispatched the same business day. Orders placed after
34        2:00 PM ET, or on weekends and federal holidays, are dispatched on the
35        next business day. During peak seasons (November 15 - December 31),
36        processing may extend by 1-2 additional business days.
37
38 Q1.5: How can I track my shipment?
39 A1.5: A tracking number is emailed to the address on file within 1 hour of
40        shipment dispatch. Tracking is available via the GigaCorp Order Portal at
41        portal.gigacorp.io/track or directly through the carrier's website.
42        GigaCorp partners with UPS, FedEx, and USPS. If no tracking update
43        appears within 48 hours of the dispatch confirmation email, contact
44        support at support@gigacorp.io with your order number.

47 SECTION 2: RETURN PROCESS
48 ---------------------------------------------------------------
49
50 Q2.1: What is GigaCorp's return window?
51 A2.1: Most items may be returned within 30 calendar days of the delivery date
52        for a full refund to the original payment method. GigaShield Pro hardware
53        devices and enterprise software licenses carry an extended 60-day return
54        window. Consumable goods (filters, ink cartridges, disposable components)
55        are non-returnable once opened. All items must be in original, unused
56        condition with all original packaging and accessories included.
57
58 Q2.2: How do I initiate a return?
59 A2.2: Log in to your account at gigacorp.io/account, navigate to "Order
60        History," select the relevant order, and click "Request Return." You will
61        receive a prepaid return shipping label via email within 2 business days.
62        Affix the label to the outside of the package and drop it off at any
63        authorized UPS or FedEx location. Returns without a GigaCorp-issued label
64        will not be accepted at our warehouse and may be returned to sender.
65
66 Q2.3: How long does the refund take to process?
67 A2.3: Once the returned item is received and inspected at our fulfillment center
68        (typically 3-5 business days after carrier pickup), refunds are issued
69        within 2 business days. Credit card and PayPal refunds typically appear
70        in 3-7 banking days. GigaWallet credits are applied instantly. Bank
71        transfer refunds may take up to 10 business days depending on the
72        receiving institution.
73
74 Q2.4: Are there any restocking fees?
75 A2.4: Standard consumer products are refunded in full with no restocking fee.
76        Enterprise hardware bundles valued over $1,500.00 are subject to a 15%
77        restocking fee if returned after 15 calendar days. Custom-configured or
78        build-to-order systems are subject to a 25% restocking fee and cannot be
79        returned after 30 days under any circumstances. Software license keys
80        that have been activated are non-refundable.
81
82 Q2.5: What if my item arrives damaged or defective?
83 A2.5: Report damaged or defective items within 7 calendar days of delivery by
84        emailing photos of the damage to defects@gigacorp.io with your order
85        number in the subject line. GigaCorp will ship a replacement unit via
86        Overnight Priority at no charge and arrange free pickup of the defective
87        item. If a replacement is unavailable, a full refund (including original
88        shipping cost) will be issued with no restocking fee applied.

94 Q3.1: When is GigaCorp customer support available?
95 A3.1: GigaCorp's general customer support operates Monday through Friday,
96        8:00 AM - 9:00 PM Eastern Time, and Saturday, 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM Eastern
97        Time. Support is closed on Sundays and all U.S. federal holidays.
98        Tier 3 (Enterprise) account holders have access to 24/7 dedicated support
99        regardless of holidays (see Section 4 for tier details).
100
101 Q3.2: How can I reach customer support?
102 A3.2: GigaCorp support is reachable through four channels: (1) Phone at
103        1-800-GIGA-HELP (1-800-444-2435), average hold time under 4 minutes;
104        (2) Live Chat at gigacorp.io/chat, available during business hours;
105        (3) Email at support@gigacorp.io, response within 1 business day;
106        (4) Community Forum at community.gigacorp.io, monitored by staff
107        and peers. Emergency escalation for Enterprise accounts: 1-800-444-9911.
108
109 Q3.3: Are GigaCorp's physical offices open to the public?
110 A3.3: GigaCorp's headquarters in Austin, TX and regional offices in Chicago, IL,
111        and Seattle, WA are open for scheduled appointments only. Walk-ins are
112        not accepted. Office hours are Monday through Friday, 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM
113        local time. To schedule an appointment, email appointments@gigacorp.io
114        at least 48 hours in advance. The Austin HQ also operates a product
115        experience showroom open Tuesday and Thursday, 10:00 AM - 4:00 PM CT.
116
117 Q3.4: Does GigaCorp observe any holidays when support is unavailable?
118 A3.4: GigaCorp's standard support team observes the following U.S. federal
119        holidays: New Year's Day, Memorial Day, Independence Day, Labor Day,
120        Thanksgiving Day, and Christmas Day. On Christmas Eve and New Year's Eve,
121        support hours are reduced to 9:00 AM - 2:00 PM ET. Enterprise (Tier 3)
122        clients are unaffected; their dedicated support line operates year-round.
123
124 Q3.5: What is the expected response time for email inquiries?
125 A3.5: Standard (Tier 1) customers can expect an email response within 1 business
126        day (24 hours during business hours). Professional (Tier 2) customers
127        receive a guaranteed 8-business-hour response. Enterprise (Tier 3)
128        customers receive a guaranteed 2-hour response at all times, including
129        nights, weekends, and holidays, with a dedicated account manager
130        assigned to every enterprise account.

133 SECTION 4: SERVICE TIERS
134 ---------------------------------------------------------------
135
136 Q4.1: What service tiers does GigaCorp offer?
137 A4.1: GigaCorp offers three service tiers: Tier 1 - Standard (free, included
138        with all accounts), Tier 2 - Professional ($49.99/month or $499.99/year),
139        and Tier 3 - Enterprise (custom pricing, starting at $1,200/month with
140        annual contract required). Each tier unlocks progressively higher support
141        priority, storage quotas, API rate limits, and dedicated resources.
142
143 Q4.2: What is included in the Standard (Tier 1) plan?
144 A4.2: The Standard plan includes: 50 GB cloud storage, up to 3 user seats,
145        standard email and chat support (business hours only), access to the
146        GigaCorp Community Forum, 1,000 API calls per day, and monthly usage
147        reports. Standard plan users are limited to 5 active projects and
148        cannot access beta features or priority processing queues.
149
150 Q4.3: What additional benefits does the Professional (Tier 2) plan provide?
151 A4.3: The Professional plan includes everything in Standard, plus: 500 GB cloud
152        storage, up to 20 user seats, 8-business-hour email support SLA, live
153        phone support during business hours, 25,000 API calls per day, advanced
154        analytics dashboard, unlimited active projects, early access to beta
155        features, and a 99.5% uptime SLA with credit compensation for outages
156        exceeding 30 minutes ($10 credit per incident, capped at $50/month).
157
158 Q4.4: What does the Enterprise (Tier 3) plan include?
159 A4.4: The Enterprise plan includes everything in Professional, plus: unlimited
160        cloud storage, unlimited user seats, 24/7 dedicated support with a named
161        account manager, 2-hour guaranteed response SLA (all hours, all days),
162        500,000 API calls per day with burst capacity up to 2,000,000 on request,
163        custom integrations and SSO/SAML configuration, on-site training sessions
164        (up to 4 per year, travel costs included), a 99.99% uptime SLA with
165        automatic credit compensation of $500 per hour of downtime, and a
166        dedicated private cloud environment option at additional cost.
167
168 Q4.5: Can I upgrade or downgrade my service tier mid-billing cycle?
169 A4.5: Upgrades take effect immediately upon confirmation. The new tier's rate is
170        prorated for the remaining days in the current billing cycle, and the
171        difference is charged to the payment method on file. Downgrades take
172        effect at the start of the next billing cycle; no partial refunds are
173        issued for the current cycle. Enterprise contracts require a 30-day
174        written notice to downgrade, submitted to contracts@gigacorp.io.
175        Users may upgrade or downgrade up to twice per calendar year without
176        a service fee; additional tier changes are subject to a $25.00 admin fee.
